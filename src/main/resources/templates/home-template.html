Welcome to the Optimistic Wanderer's blog site. This site is for the Optimistic Wanderer who loves trails and nature. This blog was created to share in our experience while using hiking trails and park in Central Ohio. We encourage all readers to blog about their experiences. If you choose to be an Optimistic Wander Blogger, I ask that you fill out all required fields. This site is family friendly, so please keep your reviews and blogs respectful.
